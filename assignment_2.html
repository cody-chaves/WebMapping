Cody Chaves Scuba History

Grand Cayman:

My first time going scuba diving was in Grand Cayman in June 2022 with my two older cousins. We did a PADI adventure dive so there was minimal training, most of it was completed on the 30 minute boat ride to the dive site. It was a beautiful day with 80F degree water and visibility from 60-80 feet. We spent most of the dive around 30 feet where we saw many stingrays, lobsters, and pufferfish.

Santa Catalina Island:

I earned my PADI open water certification in March 2023 at Catalina Island with my stepdad, we completed our fourth and final dive at Casino Point. Visibility was around 50 feet with 60F degree water and kelp forests that stretched from the seafloor to the surface. Since this was our certification dive we needed to reach 60 feet, and on the way down we spotted a seal that decided to come over and say hello!

Hoodsport, Washington:

I completed my PADI advanced open water certification in January 2026 through U of O's scuba class in Hoodsport, Washington. Over two days we completed five dives in around 40F degree water with difficult visibility ranging from nearly zero feet at the surface to 20-30 feet at depth. My favorite dive was the night dive where we turned off our flashlights and experinced bioluminescence underwater. We also got to see multiple wolf eels and giant pacific octopus which are some of my favorite animals.
